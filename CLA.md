## How to agree

**You agree to this CLA by submitting a Pull Request.**

Add your name to [CONTRIBUTORS.md](CONTRIBUTORS.md) in your PR:

```
| Your Name | GitHub @handle | date (YYYY-MM-DD) |
```

A bot will check that your name is in CONTRIBUTORS.md before the PR
can be merged.
